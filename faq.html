Frequently Asked Questions

What’s the procedure for joining surveys?

Following registration, members receive access to a list of accessible surveys. After the preliminary screening is finished, Members will be taken to the client's website to take part in surveys. We also extend invitations to members who we think MAY be eligible for a study when a customer contacts us with a survey. A login code and a link to the survey will be sent in the email. Traditionally, the survey's opening section includes a number of screening questions. You will proceed with the survey and receive payment if, in the client's opinion, you pass the screening process.

What’s the typical time required to complete a survey?

Approximately 10-15 minutes.

How do I receive my rewards?

$20 is our minimum payment amount, and you must redeem your payment in US dollars. You can convert your bizz survey points into the relevant cash amount once they reach 2000. Within 15 to 20 working days of your request, your payment will be transferred to your PayPal account. We'll get in touch with you to confirm your account, our support staff. This aids in our efforts to control fraud-related activities. Your payment request will be processed as soon as it has been validated, and if your PayPal email has been validated, payments will be made straight to your account.(With the exception of holidays and weekends).

What is the process for payment delivery?

PayPal is the most user-friendly and practical method of sending and receiving money online, and it processes 100% of payments for Bizz Survey. Go to www.paypal.com to register with PayPal. Payments will only be made by survezip to those who have a primary PayPal account confirmed in the US. For additional details, please go to Paypal.com.

What payment can I expect?

Once your Bizz survey points reach 2000, you will receive payment to your PayPal account within 15 to 20 working days of your redeem request. The minimum payment amount is $20 (2000 Bizz Survey Points). You must request payment here.

How long does it take to receive payment?

Your payment will be sent to your paypal account within 15 to 20 working days after your redeem request.

Why is it necessary to provide personal information when signing up?

We ask demographic questions such as age, gender, marital status to ensure we have a diverse, representative membership database. We only use this information to group our respondents for inclusion in appropriate surveys.

What’s the procedure to unsubscribe?

If you wish to unsubscribe, please go to Unsubscribe Link, which will remove your data from our database.

What’s the average duration that your surveys are posted?

While the average duration of a survey is two weeks, they might last longer. Some of them are live for 24 hours, while others are live for a few days. A given survey may close in a day if it receives a lot of responses. We must end the survey as soon as we have gathered the information the client requires. When we encourage you to take part in a survey, we will always let you know how much time it will take.

Is there a referral program that pays members for inviting friends?

Yes! Refer your friends to Bizz Survey and earn 15% of their survey earnings till the life of their account. For example, You refer Bob to Bizz Survey, Bob becomes a member and completes online surveys and evaluations, once he requests payment for $100 from Bizz Survey, $15 is immediately credited in your account.

Is there a specific eligibility criteria to join?

To register and be eligible for our offers, the following is required:
You must be at least 18 years old.
The account information that you provide us must be accurate and complete. You must register using your valid email address only.
Absolutely no proxy servers. These aren't allowed at all and will be traced so don't put you in rejection stage. Follow the ethics and enjoy winning at our portals.

What’s the limit on the number of accounts I can have?

You can have only ONE account per household.

Is there a specific browser I should use?

You can use any browser.

How long does the approval process for offers typically take?

When you successfully complete the surveys and get approved for reward, you will be eligible for payment. It will take minimum of two working days and up to a maximum of 1 week to get your offers approved from the advertiser.
