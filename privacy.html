Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters.

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and we are fully committed to safeguarding it. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle and protect any personal information you may provide to us, ensuring that your data is treated with the highest level of confidentiality and care.

This Privacy Policy applies specifically to this website, which is operated by Bizz Survey (“we”). It is important to note that this policy pertains solely to the information collected through this site and does not extend to other websites maintained by third parties that may be linked from our site.

Information We Collect and How We Use It.
We collect and use personal information from you primarily to respond to your requests for products and services available through the Site. For instance, when you seek quotes for insurance products, we may ask you to provide your full name, address, telephone number, email, and other relevant details. This information helps us match you with carriers and agents who can offer you appropriate services and quotes. Additionally, we collect demographic information, which includes data like gender and zip code that is not directly linked to your personal identity. Occasionally, we may receive additional information about you from online or offline sources, and this data may be added to your existing account to enhance our services.

Sharing and Disclosure of Information.
We may share your personal information under certain circumstances. If you submit a request for quotes through the Site, the information you provide (such as your name, address, and contact details) may be shared with third parties, including agents, carriers, lead aggregators, and other service providers within our network, to process your request. In some cases, we might also obtain additional information about you, such as your Social Security Number or credit report, to better match you with suitable Service Providers. Rest assured, all Service Providers in our network are bound by agreements to comply with federal and state privacy regulations. By submitting your information to us, you acknowledge that these Service Providers may contact you through various means (including phone, email, SMS, or mail) to fulfill your request, even if you are on the National Do Not Call List. If you wish not to be contacted by a particular Service Provider after your initial inquiry, you should directly request that the provider refrain from further contact.

Aggregate, Non-Personal, or De-identified Information
We may also share aggregated, non-personal, or de-identified information that does not specifically identify you with third parties, including affiliates, for purposes such as developing and delivering targeted advertising on our Site or third-party websites. This type of data is used to understand trends and preferences without revealing individual identities.

Compliance with Legal Requirements
In certain situations, we may be required to share your personal information with law enforcement or government agencies in response to subpoenas, court orders, or audits. We may also share information if it is necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, or situations involving potential threats to the safety of any person, or as otherwise required by law.

Business Transfers
As our business evolves, there may be instances where we merge with or are acquired by another company. If such a transaction occurs, the successor company would have access to the information maintained by us, including your personal data. However, the new company would remain bound by this Privacy Policy unless you consent to a new one.

Cookies and IP Address Tracking
To enhance your experience on our Site, we use "cookies," which are small pieces of code stored on your computer's hard drive. Cookies help us personalize your experience by reducing page load times and assisting with customer tracking. We may also use cookies to monitor the performance of our partners or to deliver targeted advertising on our website or third-party sites. Additionally, we track Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, which are used to identify your computer whenever you log onto the Internet.

Email Communications
You may opt-in to receive communications from us, either directly through our website or via a third party. Your email address will only be shared with our mailing partner for the purpose of sending you relevant offers and promotions. If you decide that you no longer wish to receive these communications, you can easily opt out by clicking the unsubscribe link provided in our emails.

Short Message Service (SMS)
We may offer an SMS service that allows you to receive messages on your wireless device. Data collected in connection with this service, such as your name, address, and phone number, is used in accordance with this Privacy Policy. Please note that standard messaging rates from your wireless provider may apply. The SMS service is provided on an "AS IS" basis, and we do not guarantee the privacy or security of your messages.

Third-Party Advertisers
While we control our mailing list and privacy practices, third-party advertisers may send communications that promote our services. If you wish to stop receiving emails from these advertisers, you may need to contact them directly to opt-out of their mailing lists.

Confidentiality and Security
We take numerous steps to protect the security of your personal information. Access to your data is restricted to employees or agents who need it to provide you with the products and services you request. We also maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal standards to ensure your information is secure.

Consumer Privacy Act (CPA)
We have specific rights under the Consumer Privacy Act (CPA). These rights include the ability to know what personal information is being collected about them, whether it is being sold or disclosed and to whom, the right to say no to the sale of their personal information, and the right to access or delete their personal information. If you are a California resident and wish to exercise your CCPA rights, or if you have questions about our privacy practices, please contact us as outlined in this policy.

Your Right to Opt-Out
You have the right to request that we do not sell your personal information. Personal information in this context includes sensitive data such as your name, Social Security Number, or financial account details. If you wish to make such a request, please contact us with sufficient details to verify your identity.

Categories of Information We Collect
We collect various types of information that may identify, relate to, describe, or be capable of being associated with you. This includes both personal information and other data that may be linked to your identity. The specific categories of information we collect are detailed within this Privacy Policy.
